\nI\'ve become addicted to reading these posts and sometimes I\'m shocked by all the hate. I get needing release and a way to process through feelings and what an ideal place this is for that but damn. I hated my ex for a while, but she was just someone I dated for a period of time and on this end? On this end she was just someone who wasn\'t a match REGARDLESS of our feelings, like oil and water. I think we projected alot of our hopes and desires on each other and we both carried alot of our hurts and traumas and disappointments from other relationships into this one and we mauled each other and brought each other down over things that didn\'t have anything to with each other. On this end I don\'t hate her but... She hasn\'t put it down yet. I don\'t know where the lies end and the truth begins in regards to her and her story. I was the father of her child and she wanted to work it out but that was when she was scared and alone as soon as her mother and her started getting along I was out and the fact that she ALLWAYS treated me as some less than subhuman loser I realised that I would NEVER have a relationship with my son that wasn\'t dictated and controlled by her. The fact that she promised never to use the child as a weapon and it was the first thing she did. I cannot tolerate many things about her and I find our dealings infuriating and that I REALLY do not like feeling the way I feel when I\'m dealing with her... But I really did want to coparent. All I ever wanted was a son. I here a baby cry or see an infant and I reel with instinct. The last time we talked to each other i called her with an olive branch I called to put the fight down and to make peace and clean up my side and was met with hate. The next time we saw each other was in court, and the child she said was mine she then told the court I wasn\'t the father? My point is heavy shit happens, people go to war, they lie they cheat the seek revenge. My beef is with how she treats me and talks to me and I\'m sure she could say the same about me and my behaviour with her.. REGARDLESS of wether she follows suit or not I told her I would not lash out at her and I am staying my course and will not engage. One if that child is my son I\'m not going to jeopardize his safety or happiness to get even or get my revenge, in fact REGARDLESS of who\'s child it is I\'m not going to jeopardize a mother\'s freedom or safety or put that child in harm\'s way. But the bullshit that\'s come out of this? Having to deal with the courts and the money, now that there\'s a restraining order (I hadn\'t talked to her in a month) the money it will take to deal with this, paternity test, parenting classes, supervised visitation etc... All money that could have gone for food, and diapers, and clothes... Or gas for her car. I won\'t play this game and all the hate, I\'ll stash the money away, all of it and when things change we can do the test and if he\'s mine I\'ll have something for him... It\'s just all the hatred, denying parentage or lying from the beginning to cover your tracks so your partner won\'t know you cheated? I refuse to do any of this, the truth is her and I didn\'t work and we weren\'t very nice to each other. She chooses war I chose peace... That was the offer I made thats what I stand by. Be the sound of one hand clapping if you chose but it really is alot of work standing there alone hating someone who has moved on.
