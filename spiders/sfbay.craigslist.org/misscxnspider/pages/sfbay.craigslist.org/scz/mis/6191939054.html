\nThere was a time when the journey began
\nSpring was at the door beckoning
\nWe stepped into the grass without looking back
\nEverywhere we went, gardens blossomed
\n
\nNo road was too long, no mountain could separate us
\nIn our eyes the blue sky was forever blue
\nWe wrote and rewrote our diary entries a hundred times
\nAll love is passionate
\n
\nIt was a time when even our pain was
\nSo ferrocious, so clamorous we couldn\'t disguise it
\nWe lived with fanciful dreams, innocent joys
\nBelieving our youth would last forever
\nThere would be no other love but you.
\nI was certain I knew you
\nTo be filled with hope, only to be disappointed
\n
\nI have laughed and cried for no reason
\nThe moon of youth over my head
\nBefore me a swath of deep, pure grass
\nMy dark hair has whitened
\nMy joys and sorrows are also different
\nWe ration time by seconds, minutes, hours
\nLike a miser who doesn\'t waste a penny
\nI know now that every Spring will end
\nToday the grass is green, but not so tomorrow
\nI have gone to the ends of many roads
\nOver many mountains, forests and seas.
\nI consign my dream to the page
\nMy sorrow is borne deep within
\nAnd now I love you even more.
\n
