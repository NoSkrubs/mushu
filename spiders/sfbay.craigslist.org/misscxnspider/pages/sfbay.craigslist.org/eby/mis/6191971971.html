\nOne plus one equals two. Just like when you or I met the one we love and we can learn to share our similarities/differences fairly.
\n
\nOne times one equals one. When you or I try to overcompensate our relationship with our own self interests, it doesn\'t equate to two but just one being in the relationship.
\n
\nTwo minus one equals one. When you or I fail to reconcile our differences with the other half, 1 individual person remains. One is a lonely number and two is always better than one.
\n
\nOne minus one equals zero. When we make ourselves believe we are better off alone, we deceive our true existence since many of us at one time or another, have/had the ability of conceiving love thru relations such as marriage and/or birth. How much of your our pride really defines our self worth? This equation would have to be scientifically or medically solved.
\n
\nTwo + one = three or two + two = four. Any relationship that introduces multiple non-monogamous parties of the relationship, stresses the relationship. Nevertheless, two would remain the common denominator if/when attempting to simplify into a fraction, and reduce to simplest/lowest algebraic terms.
\n
\nTwo^ (x) = x *x. Any exponential number other than one (which can substituted for x) will be > two. A relationship Is between two people. Any influences over or greater than two within the relationship will overwhelm. The two that once were the majority over their own hearts in their relationship, will become the minority over their own hearts....and subject to the hardness-neutrality-acidic influences of other people\'s hearts. I believe I just applied some chemistry here.
\n
\nTwo/one = two. Funny how relationships allows one-other(s), To divide the relationship between two people...when after doing the math, it still equates to two. That one person who divides is No differerent than maybe hiring an attorney for a divorce, or that overly critical bystander of your relationship, or seeking out a side guy or chick as a distraction. All These third party interventions are conditional and last as long as you or they allow their influence over you. If/when/however the problems in the relationship between the two is (re)solved...that\'s with or without division, the two who are in the relationship remain constant. Even after hearts, Homes, and pockets are broken in or into. So why even seek or allow division?
\n
\nOne/two= .Five. Now this relationship math is a bit tricky. This could relate to professional and/or personal Infidelity. The one person maybe now has to share his/her love with two others. It reduces that one person or that one relationship in half. Half is not a whole number.
\n
\nTwo + .five = Two.five or Two.five/two=One.two five or two.five - one = One.five. So for those who introduce children into the relationship, forget that such a blessing Creates more imbalances than it does balance. The math proves it since the addition, subtraction, division, etc examples provided, proves a whole number can not be attained (.five represents the child). Imagine the hardship imposed when the imbalances are handled while afar or apart.
\n
\nChoose love everyone! If you haven\'t, start now!!
\n
\nAll mathematical relationship equations don\'t always equate to generating an attachment. Most of those explained above equate to judgements, divisions, and separations. Can\'t you see?
